Molecule: Spectral line transition data for various molecules
==============================================================

For fitting of excitation diagrams, transition data (energy levels, wavelengths, temperatures, etc.) are manage via subclasses of the :class:`~pdrtpy.molecule.BaseMolecule` class.  Currently, :math:`H_2, ^{12}CO, ^{13}CO, ^{12}C^{18}O, ^{13}C^{18}O, {\rm and}~ CH^{+}` are supported.
